Projects

Portable Shower Kit
Portable Shower Kit

This was the second group project we did for my makerlab class. We decided on creating a portable shower kit to help the homeless community here in Brantford. We went through a lot of different stages in the building process in order to find what works and what doesn't. We decided on sewing as our main method for attaching the different parts of the bag. We used things like an emergancy heater to solve the problem of the water temprature during the coolder seasons.

Cardbord Shelf
Cardbord Shelf

This was the first project we did as a group for makerlab. We needed to find a way to upcycle something used by students into something useful. We decided on a cardbord shelf due to the fact that majority of students are moving into Brantford from other cities. They are taking thier belongings in things like cardbord boxes. Something we beleive is useful that most people would like in their room was a shelf, so that was our reasoning behind making it. We added hooks to the side to make it more versatile. We did face some difficulty when brainstorming how we would strengthen the shelves but overcame it by adding more cardbord below each shelf for support.

Sitemap

These tests that we conducted allowed us to gain a good scope of what was needed to be fix and what re-deisgns would be useful to present to the organization of the website.
